Our Story & Passion for Culinary Excellence

At Ember & Stone, we combine tradition and innovation to deliver unforgettable dining experiences. Join us as we take you through our journey, our chefs, and our signature creations.

Our Journey

2010 - The Beginning

Ember & Stone was founded with a vision to bring a modern twist to classic dishes while celebrating local flavors.

2015 - First Milestone

We opened our second branch and started hosting culinary workshops for aspiring chefs and food enthusiasts.

2020 - Recognition

Our dedication to quality earned us national recognition and awards for innovation in dining experiences.
